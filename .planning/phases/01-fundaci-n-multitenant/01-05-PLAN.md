<objective>
Prove the foundation actually holds: seed two test tenants (D-16) and verify — against the live database — cross-tenant isolation (RLS), timezone correctness, and DB-level double-booking rejection. Establish the mandatory `tenantScoped(tenantId)` query layer for the bot's service_role path (CORE-03) with a functional smoke check (the formal cross-tenant service_role suite, SEC-03, is deferred to Phase 7).

Augmenting `apps/bot` deps here does NOT regress the wave-1 arm64 proof (plan 02): that gate is point-in-time and both packages are pure-JS / arm64-safe.

Purpose: Turn the four data-integrity Success Criteria from "structurally present" into "behaviorally verified with real data," and lay down the query-discipline pattern that prevents the single-missing-`WHERE` cross-tenant leak (Pitfall 7) before any bot logic exists.
Output: declared+installed deps; `supabase/seed.sql`; three verify scripts; `apps/bot/src/db/{client,tenantScoped}.ts` + its test.
</objective>
